## Risk Management

### Launch Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Server overload** | Medium | High | Pre-scale infrastructure, have rollback plan |
| **Critical bugs on launch** | Medium | High | 2-week beta, staged rollout |
| **Low sign-up volume** | Medium | Medium | Reserve paid ads budget, influencer backup |
| **Negative press/reviews** | Low | High | Rapid response team, transparent communication |
| **Competitor announcement** | Low | Medium | Focus on unique features (Custom RAG, RU market) |
| **Support overwhelm** | High | Medium | FAQ, chatbot, prioritize paying users |

### Contingency Plans

**If sign-ups < 50% target by midday:**
- Activate paid ads immediately (₽100K budget)
- Ask influencers for additional posts
- Send reminder to waitlist non-openers

**If critical bug discovered:**
- Communicate transparently
- Rollback if needed
- Offer compensation to affected users

**If negative review goes viral:**
- Respond within 1 hour
- Address concerns publicly
- Reach out to author directly
